About

TextEasy is a free BBCode editor created by justAboxTV and _MajorLazer_. This website includes tools to make any Enjin post look fresh to death. We guarantee that you will never go back to the old-fashoned way of posting again!

Turn any stale or boring post into a piece of art. Whether you're an average Enjin user or the owner of some big server, you'll end up falling in love with the simplicity and usability of our app! (Also works on forum post.)

Team

TextEasy is a free BBCode editor created by justAboxTV, _MajorLazer_ and Mr_Narwhal. This website was inspired by the time and hassle it takes to make a nice looking post on Enjin. We hope that you'll never have to deal with the trouble it takes to make a nice looking post on Enjin again!

The Dev team consist of justAboxTV and _MajorLazer_. And the art was created by Mr_Narwhal and justAboxTV. We hope you enjoy our simple web app!
